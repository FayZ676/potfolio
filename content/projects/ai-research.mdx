**Frameworks and Languages**: Python

**Libraries**: ScikitLearn, Numpy, Pandas, Spacy, sciSpacy, Metamap, Matplotlib

Conducted and published peer-reviewed research analyzing 1164 COVID-19 news articles using advanced machine learning algorithms to extract 158 novel features and train 15 machine learning classifiers to predict COVID-19 fake news with over 90% accuracy and over 30% greater accuracy compared to models trained on traditional features.
